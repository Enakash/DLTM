Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

LIEDAR (“the App,” “we,” “our”) is a personal voice-analysis tool developed by an independent developer operating as an exempt dealer (עוסק פטור) in Israel under the business name Liedar. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is processed, and your rights regarding that data.

This App is intended as a personal speech-feedback and self-practice tool — for example, rehearsing presentations, monitoring your own vocal tone, or reviewing your own speech patterns. It is not a lie-detection device and makes no claims of scientific accuracy in detecting truthfulness. See the Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Microphone Audio

The App requests access to your device’s microphone (RECORD_AUDIO permission) solely to analyze vocal characteristics in real time, including pitch, jitter (voice micro-tremor), speech pace, pauses, volume, and breathing rate.

1.2 What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not record, save, or export raw audio at any time.
  • We do not transmit audio or analysis data to any server. All processing happens entirely on your device.
  • We do not use cloud storage, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or device identifiers.

1.3 Locally Stored Data

If you choose to save a session summary (a numeric credibility score, timestamp, and call duration) and label it with a contact name you type in yourself, that information is stored only in the App’s local storage on your device, using Android’s standard SharedPreferences mechanism. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not accessible to us, to the developer, or to any third party.
  • Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App, or manually if you delete a contact within the App.

2. How We Use Information

All voice analysis is performed locally and in real time using on-device signal processing (autocorrelation-based pitch detection and related acoustic measurements). Results are displayed to you immediately and are not retained unless you explicitly choose to save a session summary as described in Section 1.3.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any data to third parties, because no data is ever transmitted off your device. There are no analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs integrated into this App.

4. Children’s Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect any information from children, as no information is collected from any user.

5. Third-Party Voice Analysis

The App is designed for analyzing your own voice as a self-practice and feedback tool. If you use the App in a setting where another person’s voice may be picked up by the microphone (for example, during a phone call on speaker), you are responsible for ensuring you have the legal right to do so under the laws applicable to you, including any wiretapping, eavesdropping, or two-party consent laws in your jurisdiction. The developer provides this tool for personal, single-party use and assumes no liability for how it is used. See the Terms of Use for further detail.

6. Permissions Used

  • RECORD_AUDIO — required to perform real-time voice analysis.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE — required so analysis can continue while the screen is on and the app is active.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — used to show a persistent notification while analysis is running, as required by Android for foreground microphone services.
  • VIBRATE — used for optional haptic alerts during analysis.

7. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, the primary security boundary is your device’s own lock screen and operating system protections. We recommend keeping your device’s OS updated and using a secure lock method.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can deny or revoke microphone access at any time via Android Settings → Apps → LIEDAR → Permissions.
  • You can delete any saved contact/session data directly within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
