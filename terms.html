1. What LIEDAR Is — and What It Is Not

LIEDAR analyzes acoustic features of voice audio in real time — including pitch, micro-tremor (jitter), pause patterns, speech rate, volume, and breathing rate — and presents a composite “credibility” score along with a plain-language explanation of which features contributed to that score.

IMPORTANT: LIEDAR is NOT a scientifically validated lie-detection device. No acoustic or physiological signal — including those measured by this App — has been scientifically proven to reliably distinguish truthful statements from false ones. Voice stress analysis technology, including the techniques used in this App, is widely regarded by researchers and forensic bodies as having insufficient scientific reliability for use as evidence of deception.

The App is intended as a personal self-awareness and speech-practice tool. Example uses include:

  • Practicing presentations, interviews, or public speaking and reviewing your own vocal stability.
  • Becoming more aware of your own stress indicators while speaking.
  • Informal, non-evidentiary self-reflection on your communication patterns.

LIEDAR must not be used, and may not be relied upon, for:

  • Determining the truthfulness of any statement made by yourself or another person.
  • Employment screening, background checks, or any hiring/firing decision.
  • Legal proceedings, evidence gathering, investigations, or law enforcement purposes.
  • Medical, psychological, or psychiatric diagnosis of any kind.
  • Any decision with legal, financial, medical, or safety consequences for yourself or another person.
